This data sub directory contains synthetic fertilizer use estimates available in the following repositories (but organized here for ease of use):

Einarsson, Rasmus; Sanz-Cobena, Alberto; Aguilera, Eduardo; Billen, Gilles; Garnier, Josette; van Grinsven, Hans JM; et al. (2021): Crop production and nitrogen use in European cropland and grassland 1961-2019. figshare. Collection. https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.c.5320772.v1 

Ludemann, Cameron; Gruere, Armelle; Heffer, Patrick; Dobermann, Achim (2022), Global data on fertilizer use by crop and by country, Dryad, Dataset, https://doi.org/10.5061/dryad.2rbnzs7qh

Zou, Tan et al. (2021), Nitrogen budget data, Dryad, Dataset, https://doi.org/10.5061/dryad.vt4b8gtrd
